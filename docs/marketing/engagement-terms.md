## Engagement terms

Two ways to start. No middle tier — pick the one that fits.

### Lite — $7,500 setup (primary)

The default offer. Designed for operators who want to get on the platform fast, prove revenue lift, and avoid retainer lock-in while they're still learning what the tooling can do.

- **$7,500 one-time setup.** Covers tenant provisioning, brand theming, the standard module stack wired to your data, and a working AI feature set on day one.
- **No mandatory retainer for the first 90 days.** Use the platform, see what it does, decide later.
- **15% commission on sponsor revenue and leads sourced through the platform.** We only make money when the platform makes you money. Tracked transparently in the dashboard.
- **Case-study rights.** We can publish results, screenshots, and a written case study after 90 days. Anonymized on request; named is preferred.
- **Support cadence:** async (Slack / email), 1 standing weekly check-in for the first 30 days, then on-demand.

### Full — $25,000 setup + retainer (secondary)

For operators who want hands-on white-glove integration — custom modules, deeper data migration, embedded help running the system day-to-day. Most clients don't need this. Start with Lite if you're not sure.

- **$25,000 one-time setup.** Includes everything in Lite plus custom module work, full data migration from existing CRM / spreadsheet stack, branded customer-facing surfaces, and integration wiring beyond the standard vendors.
- **Monthly retainer required.** Scoped to engagement; typically covers ongoing build, AI tuning, and operator-side execution.
- **No commission component.** Straight retainer relationship.
- **Case-study rights negotiable.** Default is non-public; named case study available as a discount lever.
- **Support cadence:** dedicated, multiple touchpoints per week, embedded in your operations.

### What's not on offer

No middle tier. No "$15k starter" or "$10k base + add-ons." Two clean options keeps the decision honest — if Lite isn't enough, you need Full. Halfway is how scope creep and bad fits happen.

Usage-based costs (Anthropic, AssemblyAI, Apify, etc.) are passed through at cost on both tiers. Neither tier includes unbounded API usage.
